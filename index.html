About Jack Slack

History

Jack Slack has been writing about combat sports and martial arts since 2012 and has worked for a number of online and print publications. He is best known for his work with Vice's Fightland channel and his first paperback, Notorious: The Life and Fights of Conor McGregor.

Mission & Vision

Mission & Vision

The Fight Primer is a website devoted to the study and analysis of the fight game.

Latest Articles
